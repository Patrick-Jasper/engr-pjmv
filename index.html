About Me

Engr. Patrick Jasper M. Villareal is a licensed civil engineer and master plumber, currently working as a Structural Design Engineer for Seven Peaks Design and Build, Inc. He has also previously taken positions as a Site Engineer and Health, Environment, and Safety (HES) Coordinator. As a structural engineer, he is adept in structural design, structural assessment, and retrofitting designs, completing projects ranging from designing low-rise residential buildings to conducting retrofitting studies for an 11-storey medical school building.

Engr. Villareal is a Bachelor's degree holder in Civil Engineering from the University of the Philippines Los Baños. He is also currently studing for his Diploma in Computer Science at the University of the Philippines Open University. Furthermore, he also plans to pursue Master's studies in Structural Engineering.
